The primary goal of the chart below is to present a visual representation of the stock prices of various companies. The chart helps display and compare the stock prices of different companies in a visually appealing and understandable format. Relationship between the market capitalization (market cap) and the forward price-to-earnings ratio (forward P/E ratio) of different companies

The bubble chart below is intended to visualize the relationship between the market capitalization (market cap) and the forward price-to-earnings ratio (forward P/E ratio) of different companies. Each bubble in the chart represents a company, where its position on the x-axis corresponds to the company symbol, its position on the y-axis corresponds to its market cap, and its size (radius) represents the forward P/E ratio. The chart allows users to compare and analyze the market cap and valuation metrics of multiple companies simultaneously, making it easier to identify patterns and trends in their financial performance.
