Insider Trading Transactions

Form Screener

The bubble chart below visualizes insider trading activity for various companies in a market. Each bubble in the chart represents an individual insider and is positioned based on the average price per share they transacted and the number of shares they own. The size of each bubble is determined by the total value of their transactions. This visualization helps to identify patterns and trends in insider trading behavior, understand the distribution of transactions across different companies, and possibly gain insights into how insiders' actions may impact the market sentiment or company performance.
