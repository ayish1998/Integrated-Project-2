Empowering Ghanaian MSMEs: Baabuh Hub's Digital Skills Training Webinar for Business Growth

On 15th December 2022, Baabuh Hub organised a webinar focused on digital skills training for MSMEs (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) in Ghana. The webinar aimed to equip entrepreneurs with the necessary digital skills required to effectively operate in the modern business environment. With the COVID-19 pandemic accelerating the adoption of digital technology, it has become more critical than ever for MSMEs to be able to leverage digital tools to remain competitive and sustainable. The webinar was well-attended by entrepreneurs from various sectors of the Ghanaian economy, including agriculture, manufacturing, services, and technology.

Baabuh Hub Opens its Remote Internship Program

In today's rapidly evolving digital world, it has become increasingly important for individuals to possess practical skills that are relevant to the current job market. However, gaining real-world experience and practical knowledge can be challenging, particularly for students or those who are just starting their careers. That is where remote internship opportunities come in. Remote internships allow individuals to gain practical skills and experience from the comfort of their own homes, making it a convenient option for those who cannot attend traditional in-person internships.

Baabuh Hub Secures Funding and Mentorship Support from GhanaThink Foundation and Digital Opportunity Trust

Baabuh Hub, a technology company based in Ghana, has recently won the Komseko Social Enterprise Program run by GhanaThink and Digital Opportunity Trust. Baabuh Hub is a startup social enterprise that provides digital solutions to small businesses in Ghana, empowering them to grow and succeed in today's increasingly digital world.
